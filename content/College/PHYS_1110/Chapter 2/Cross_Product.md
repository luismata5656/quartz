# Cross Product using a Matrix

the formula for the cross product can be derived using the derterminant of the Matrix

$$A = ai + bj + ck $$
$$B = xi + yj + zk $$

Thus,

$$ A \times B = \begin{vmatrix} i & j & k \\ a & b & c \\ x & y & z \end{vmatrix} $$
